# Fun Facts About AI

**1. AI Can Dream (Sort Of)**
Modern neural networks like GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) generate entirely new images, music, and text by learning patterns — a process eerily similar to human imagination and dreaming.

**2. The First AI Program Was Written in 1951**
Long before the term "artificial intelligence" was coined, Alan Turing's student Christopher Strachey wrote a checkers-playing program in 1951 — considered one of the earliest working AI programs in history.

**3. AI Can Predict Earthquakes**
Researchers have used deep learning models to analyze seismic data and predict aftershocks with greater accuracy than traditional methods, potentially saving thousands of lives in disaster-prone regions.
